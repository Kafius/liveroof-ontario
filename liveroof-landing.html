The Best of Both Worlds in Green Roofing

LiveRoof offers the natural function and natural beauty of built-in-place living roofs — without the extensive ongoing costs of maturation. It's the only system that delivers an instantly mature, fully vegetated green roof.

Unlike conventional mat or carpet systems, LiveRoof modules arrive pre-grown, interconnected, and ready to thrive — eliminating the establishment phase that costs time and money.
